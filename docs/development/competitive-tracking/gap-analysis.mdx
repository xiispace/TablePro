## Recommendations

The biggest cluster is **database driver** gaps (9 of 20 items): Athena (#2798, #2706), InfluxDB (#1734), Snowflake (#1150), Firebird (#790), and the broad "analytics databases" ask (#223). Each is bounded to one new plugin bundle; the system already ships 17 drivers. Athena and InfluxDB are the highest-scoring single missing drivers.

**Linux** (#1056) and **Windows and Linux** (#9) top the list by score but are out of scope: TablePro is native macOS and iOS by design. They stay in the table as demand signal, not as planned work.

The two non-driver feature gaps are **drag-to-reorder columns in Structure view** (#607) and **CHECK constraints in Structure view** (#1118). Both are bounded data-grid work and also show up in [quick wins](/development/competitive-tracking/quick-wins).

Several closed entries are **bug classes** worth a regression check: connection list vanishing (#83), idle memory growth (#1879), Postgres-over-SSH regression (#1275), and off-screen windows on multi-display setups (#2015). TablePlus closed them; confirm TablePro is not hit by the same class.

Total Missing items in this dataset: 239. The 20 above are the high-impact ones; the long tail (`score < 30`) sits in [by category](/development/competitive-tracking/by-category) for opportunistic pickup.
